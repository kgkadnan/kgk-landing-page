Traceability Programs We Offer

To request a traceability program for any stone, please get in touch with our sales team.

GIA Source Verification Service (SVS)

GIA, a trusted gem institute, verifies the reported source country of polished diamonds using existing documents and audits participating manufacturers. This program boosts consumer confidence by adding a layer of verification. It relies on documentation accuracy, GIA SVS is a valuable step towards a more transparent diamond industry.

DB Origin & Origin Story by De Beers

Origin Story is an extension of De Beers' broader Origin suite, which combines diamond traceability from source (powered by TRACR) with information about the diamond's impact on people and the planet. De Beers Origin Story goes beyond just providing basic traceability data. It aims to create a more engaging experience for retailers and ultimately, consumers.

Everledger

Everledger uses blockchain technology to create a traceability program for diamonds, aiming to bring transparency & trust to the diamond industry. The program tracks a diamond's journey from mine to consumer, recording details like origin, characteristics, & ownership history on a secure blockchain ledger that creates an immutable record of the diamond's journey.

Tracr by De Beers

TRACR is a leading traceability platform developed by De Beers Group. It utilizes a combination of technologies like blockchain, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to track a diamond's journey from mine to market.
