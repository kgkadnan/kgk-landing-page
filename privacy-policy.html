  • This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide or that we collect through your use of our Website. It explains how and under what circumstances your information is managed.

4. What information do we collect about you?

Personal information you provide voluntarily:

  • Information provided via Website forms, such as account registration, order details, or the “Raise an Enquiry” form

  • Social media, web chat, and ‘WhatsApp’ chat interactions with information like name, email, and call recordings.

Other Incidental Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device information, usage details, IP addresses, and browsing patterns.
  • Cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.
  • Data on website visits, traffic, and location data.

Our Website does not require sensitive information like passwords, bank details, or medical history for account registration or product viewing. However, some users may need to provide financial details directly to product owners for transactions. KGK is not responsible for the storage or management of sensitive information shared with product owners.

Personal information you provide voluntarily:

  • It is important to note that the Website does not require you to provide any ‘sensitive’ personal information, which may include your passwords, financial information such as bank account number, credit card/ debit card number or other payment instruction details, medical records and history, biometric information, sexual orientation, etc., for the purpose of registering your account, viewing the products on the Website, contacting us, or raising an enquiry

By consenting to this Privacy Policy, you acknowledge and accept that the Company is not, and shall never be, liable for the retention, transfer, storage, management and/or use of this ‘sensitive’ personal information which would be provided by You directly to the product owner, without any intervention by the Company.
